Dental Cleaning

Dental Cleaning involves the removal of plaque, tartar (calculus), and stains from the teeth. This procedure helps prevent gum disease and cavities, promoting overall oral health.

Filling

Dental fillings are used to repair teeth that have been damaged due to decay or minor fractures. The decayed or damaged portion of the tooth is removed, and the resulting cavity is filled with materials like composite resin, amalgam, or porcelain.

Root Canal

Root canal treatment is performed when the pulp inside a tooth becomes infected or inflamed due to deep decay, repeated dental procedures, or trauma. During the procedure, the infected pulp is removed, and the root canals are thoroughly cleaned, disinfected, and sealed with a filling material. This saves the tooth from extraction and relieves pain.

Dental Implant

Dental implants are a permanent solution for replacing missing teeth. They involve the surgical placement of titanium posts into the jawbone, which act as artificial tooth roots. These implants fuse with the bone over time, providing a stable foundation for attaching crowns, bridges, or dentures on top

Dental Braces

Dental Braces are used to correct misaligned teeth and improper bites. Braces consist of metal brackets attached to the teeth, connected with wires, while aligners are clear, removable trays that gradually shift teeth into proper alignment. Orthodontic treatment enhances dental aesthetics, improves oral health, and aids in better jaw function.
